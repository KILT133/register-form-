Admissions Open at Karnataka Institute of Leather & Fashion Technology (KILT), Bengaluru

KILT invites applications for admission to Diploma in Leather & Fashion Technology (3 years), and various short-term certificate courses. Candidates who have passed SSLC (10th standard) are eligible to apply, and 2nd PUC (Science) passed students are eligible for direct admission to the 2nd year of the course.

All programs are affiliated with the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Government of Karnataka, and approved by AICTE. Admission is based on merit and available seats, with priority given to early applicants. Interested students can apply by clicking the "Apply Now" button below.

Required Documents

  1. SSLC/PUC Markscard
  2. Caste/Income certificate
  3. Passport-size photographs

For more details regarding application deadlines, fee structure, and counseling dates, contact the institute office directly.
